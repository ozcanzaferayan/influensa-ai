# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2026

Influensa AI (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Influensa AI mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect:
- Your Google account email address
- Your display name (if available)
- A unique Firebase user ID (UID)

We **do not** collect or store your Google password.

---

### 1.2 User-Generated Content
When you use the App to generate images, we collect and store:
- Text prompts you provide
- Selected options (e.g. ethnicity, hair color, scene)
- Optional product reference images (if uploaded)
- AI-generated images created for your account

These are stored securely and associated only with your user account.

---

### 1.3 Usage Data
We may collect limited technical data for functionality and abuse prevention, such as:
- Image generation timestamps
- Credit usage counts
- App version and platform (iOS / Android)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Authenticate users securely
- Generate AI-based influencer images
- Store and display your generated content
- Manage credit usage and prevent abuse
- Improve app stability and performance
- Comply with legal and platform requirements

---

## 3. AI Image Generation

Influensa AI uses third-party AI services to generate images based on user input.

Important notes:
- Generated images are **artificially created** and do not represent real individuals
- The App does **not** generate images of minors
- Explicit or sexual content is strictly prohibited
- Product reference images are used only to visually guide generation and are not redesigned or altered

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- Authentication is handled by **Firebase Authentication**
- Generated images are stored in **Firebase Storage**
- Metadata is stored in **Firebase Firestore**
- All data is protected using industry-standard security practices

Access to user data is restricted and isolated per account.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

Data may be shared only with:
- Google Firebase services (authentication, storage, database)
- AI service providers strictly for image generation

These services process data solely to provide app functionality.

---

## 6. Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active
- Generated images remain available unless deleted by you or your account is removed
- You may request deletion of your data at any time

---

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal data
